## WiFi Presence Rules
- **Latency:** 5-15+ minutes for roaming, not instant
- **Use for:** HVAC hints, away mode, general trends
- **Don't use for:** Motion triggers, room-level automations
- **Requirements:** Primary SSID, no MAC randomization, 802.11r OFF
